programs

School feeding program

HOT MEALS
These are meals that are served to children per plate on an ordinary school day either from the school kitchen or a central kitchen using an an NFC card.this system helps the schools in planning for meals,ordering for food and cooking materials like firewood etc.It helps in maintaining good health,indiscipline in school, reducing absenteeism and improving scores. The school feeding program ensures that the cooked food is safe and nutritious. In special schools for learners with Autism, Diabetes, Cerebral palsy and other neuro-diverse conditions, special consideration on the type of food,the way it's prepared in advance before cooking is crucial.This is because of the negative effects food can cause to these children.

NUTRITION BAR

This is a compressed bar of different foods mixed together each food with a different nutritional value meant to take care of malnourished children,children living far away from fresh food markets,those in the slum areas and other regions where road networks and other forms of communication is poor.These food has a long shelf life,well packaged,easy to handle,distribute and store.children can chew it easily drink water,milk or simply relax after the meal.

FOOD SUPPLIERS

M-sifa has identified certified food processors and suppliers who can comfortably avail either hot meals or nutritional bars to children both in regular schools, special schools (SNE and to homes of Orphans, Refugees, and other vulnerable children (OVCs).using real time technologies that are reliable,user friendly and easy use.
